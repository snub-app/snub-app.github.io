SNUB — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2025

Welcome to SNUB. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. SNUB is an anonymous, location-based confession app. We take privacy and safety seriously — below is how we handle data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data you provide

  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Anonymous user identifier (for example: abc@snub.app)
  • Confessions, moods, categories, hashtags you post
  • Reports you submit about other posts

1.2 Automatically collected data

  • Approximate location (only if you grant permission)
  • Device information (OS, model, app version)
  • Usage analytics (app opens, feature usage, session length)
  • Crash logs and performance metrics

1.3 Content you post

All confessions, comments, likes and related metadata are stored in our database. Confessions may be set to auto-delete after 24 hours based on app rules.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve SNUB (deliver features, show local confessions).
  • To enforce age restrictions and moderation policies.
  • To detect spam, abuse, or other policy violations.
  • To analyse and improve app performance and user experience.

3. Moderation & Safety

We use automated and manual moderation to keep SNUB safe:

  • Automated filters & AI moderation (e.g. OpenAI moderation models)
  • Custom profanity lists (English, Tamil, other supported languages)
  • Community reporting and human review for flagged content

If content is flagged as harmful, we may hide it, mark it as needs_review, or remove it entirely.

4. Location Data

Location is optional. If you enable it, we use approximate location to show nearby confessions. We do not store precise GPS coordinates permanently without explicit consent.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only in limited situations:

  • With law enforcement where required by law
  • With service providers and partners that help operate the app (they must follow confidentiality rules)

6. Data Retention

Confessions are generally set to auto-delete after 24 hours. Data necessary for moderation, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or legal compliance may be retained longer as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request:

  • Access to your data
  • Deletion of your account and data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of analytics or marketing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@snub.app

8. Children’s Privacy

SNUB is for users 17 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If we become aware of a minor using the service, we will remove the account and related content.

9. Security

We store data securely in Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Storage) and use industry-standard protections. However, no system is 100% secure — we maintain monitoring and incident response procedures.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will show the date of last update and, where required, notify users of material changes.
