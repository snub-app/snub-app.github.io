FAQ

1. I found harmful or abusive content. What should I do?
Use the Report button under any confession. Reported content will be reviewed and may be removed.

2. Is my identity truly anonymous?
Yes. Snub does not collect personal identity like phone number or email. Your chosen username and activity are anonymized.

3. Why can't I see some confessions?
Confessions with high reports or deleted by the poster will no longer appear.

4. How does location work?
Snub uses approximate location only. Exact GPS is never stored. Your identity is never linked to location.
