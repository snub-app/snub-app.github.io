Snub – Account & Data Deletion

Snub respects your privacy and gives you full control over your account and data. You may request permanent deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Snub app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

Once confirmed, your account deletion request is processed immediately.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Anonymous user ID
  • All confessions and comments posted by you
  • Likes, reports, blocks, and streak data
  • Associated metadata linked to your account

Data Retention

All personal and account-related data is permanently deleted. Some anonymized or aggregated data may be retained for legal, security, or analytics purposes for up to 30 days.
