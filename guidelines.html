Snub — Community Guidelines

These rules keep Snub safe, honest, and respectful.

Be Respectful

Share your thoughts honestly, but never at the expense of others. Treat people with respect. Abuse and harassment are not tolerated.

What is prohibited

  • Harassment & Bullying: No targeted attacks, repeated harassment, threats or intimidation.
  • Hate Speech: No content that attacks a protected class or uses hate symbols.
  • Sexually Explicit Material: No pornographic content or explicit sexual acts.
  • Doxxing & Private Info: Never share private information about others (phone, address, ID).
  • Violent or Illegal Activity: No admission of intent to commit crimes or instructions to harm.
  • Self-harm Encouragement: No praising or encouraging self-harm or suicide.

Reporting & Enforcement

If you encounter content that violates these guidelines:

  • Long-press a confession to report it.
  • Tap a username to block or report a user.
  • Reports are reviewed and acted upon within 24 hours.
  • Repeated offenders may be suspended or permanently banned.
If someone is in immediate danger: contact your local emergency services. Snub is not an emergency service and cannot replace professional help.

How Snub uses moderation

We use keyword filters, AI moderation tools, and human review to detect and remove harmful content. Some content is blocked before it appears; other content is removed after reports.
